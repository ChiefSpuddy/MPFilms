Surface Protection on Vessel ("SPV")

We offer all of these Hullguard and Slipguard surface protection products, from the Nitto SPV range, on lightweight reels for ease of handling or in custom sizes up to 1.5m wide for some products.

We can also supply 2-inch tension control core handles to allow for ease of application on the variable angled surfaces often found in marine applications.

It is important to note that all our films and tapes are designed to be applied to a dry and dust-free surface.

Application kits (which include MPF squeegee, film cutters and core handles) are available.

Hullguard Protect (blue or clear)

These are general purpose surface protection films of 0.05mm thick, with excellent tack - the only difference being the clear or blue finish to the roll. They both offer Nitto's renowned Special Adhesive Technology so, when applied to clean, dry surfaces, they will stay put. When using our special application kits along with a comfortable roll size, both options are very easy to apply, with low unwind and excellent adhesive qualities. Hullguard Premium

This premium surface protection film really is the top product for all your needs, both inside and out! The Hullguard Premium is a unique white polyolefin film, (available in two formats, MPF701 and MPF724) offering protection to gelcoat and high gloss finished surfaces during construction, refit or winter layup. Hullguard Premium films have the ability to perform equally well on interior and exterior surfaces (provided they are applied correctly to clean and dry surfaces). The Premium MPF701 is the ONLY marine film we know of that's tested and approved for 12 months weather resistance and UV protection (the MPF724 is tested for up to 9 months UV weatherability) as well as for protection against surface oxydation. What's more, there is no adhesive transfer and no contamination. Having up to 600% elongation, both options stretch over and around those changing hull, deck and superstructure designs. Both Hullguard Premium options are recyclable.

Application Information

Proper Application For Best Results

  • 1

    Surface Preparation

    Ensure all surfaces are clean, dry and dust-free before application. Any contaminants will affect adhesion.

  • 2

    Temperature Conditions

    Apply films at temperatures between 10°C and 35°C for optimal adhesion properties.

  • 3

    Handling

    Use 2-inch tension control core handles for easier application, especially on angled surfaces.

  • 4

    Smoothing

    Use the MPF squeegee to remove air bubbles and ensure proper adhesion to the surface.
