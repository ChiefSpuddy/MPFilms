MARINE-SEAL

Ref-SC140

MARINE-SEAL (Ref-SC140) is a tape designed for corrosion protection on support structures found under floorboards and cargo panels within a vessel. Common areas of use include galleys, lavatories, door sills and more. MARINESEAL exhibits strong adhesion to most substrates and is easy to remove during maintenance checks. Its smooth PTFE backing allows for easy installation and workability.

A REACH and RoHS-compliant, single sided tape that is easy to install, offering corrosion protection through extended 3,000-hour salt spray tests.

Strong adhesion to most substrates and easy to remove during maintenance checks.

Its smooth PTFE backing allows for easy installation and workability.

  • Excellent workability
  • Lightweight design that helps contribute to a more fuel-efficient boat
  • Weatherability: Offers corrosion protection through extended 3,000-hour salt spray tests
  • Removes easily for a more efficient tear down
  • Environmentally-friendly FR system(No RoHs, REACH hazard material) (No Halogen, No antimony trioxide)

MARINE-SEAL 2

Ref-DC260

MARINE-SEAL 2 (Ref-DC260), is a double-coated, differential adhesion pressure sensitive adhesive tape that utilizes a unique silicone rubber and polyether adhesive construction to protect against moisture penetration on flat or irregular surfaces in marine applications.

Exhibits strong adhesion on the adhesive side and moderate adhesion on the silicone rubber side, meanwhile both sides leave no residue upon removal.

Reach and RoHS compliant, the silicone rubber is 100% cured, highly hydrophobic, and low durometer to allow for easy conformability and rework ability while also preventing moisture penetration and migration.

Easy installation and workability

  • Use: Compressible construction allows for efficient sealing where gasket materials are needed
  • Silicone rubber is 100% cured, highly hydrophobic, and low durometer to allow for easy conformability and rework ability
  • Prevents moisture penetration and migration
  • Easy installation and workability
  • Available in custom sizes if required (price & availability upon application)
  • Compliance: Reach and RoHS

BARRIER TAPE

Ref-11611MB

Make the maintenance process easier with BARRIER TAPE (Ref-11611MB) which is a 16 mil polyurethane moisture barrier tape, with 2 mil adhesive, for floor panels of certain vessels, creating a barrier to prevent moisture and liquids from being able to cause corrosion. It is both highly flame retardant and extremely resistant to a variety of liquids.

Highly puncture and tear resistant for increased effectiveness of the moisture barrier. Low water vapour transmission rate, with high elongation to make it more forgiving in active workplaces.

Using the benefit of a two-part application, BARRIER TAPE first applies to the floor panel seams, then is applied to the whole floor panel to help prevent the passing of moisture.

  • Use: Designed with a single release liner that creates less waste and reduces handling time
  • Excellent workability allows for easy repositioning and application over irregular shapes and gaps
  • Low water vapor transmission rate
  • High elongation makes it more forgiving in active workplaces
  • A moisture barrier tape, with a 2-mil adhesive
  • Compliance: Reach and RoHS
