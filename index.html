Our Solutions

Surface Protection

We offer a range of surface protection films from the Nitto SPV range, available on lightweight reels or in custom sizes up to 1.5m wide. Application is made easier with optional 2-inch tension control core handles — ideal for the varied angles found on yachts.

All films are designed for dry, dust-free surfaces and can be applied using our dedicated MPF kits, which include a squeegee, film cutters, and core handles.

Specialised Tapes

Our marine-grade adhesive tapes are supplied on lightweight reels or in custom sizes up to 1.5m wide. To support precision during application on complex surfaces, we also offer 2-inch tension control core handles.

All tapes must be applied to clean, dry surfaces, and are compatible with our application kits — including MPF squeegee, film cutters, and more.

Sealing Materials

All sealing solutions are offered on lightweight reels or custom sizes, with tools like 2-inch core handles available to aid application across irregular marine surfaces.

For best results, surfaces must be clean and dry before use. MPF application kits are available and include a squeegee, film cutters, and core handles for a professional finish.
