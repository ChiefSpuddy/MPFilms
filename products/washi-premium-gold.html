Precision Masking for Superyacht Finishing (MPF7288)

Washi PREMIUM Gold is a high-performance fine line masking tape engineered with Nitto's proprietary Washi Tape technology — developed specifically for the demands of marine painting and detailing.

At just 0.09mm thick, it provides razor-sharp paint break lines on both smooth and irregular surfaces, making it perfect for curved or complex contours on luxury vessels. This tape performs flawlessly indoors and outdoors, offering up to 6 months of UV and weather resistance, even in hot environments.

It doesn't tear on removal, won't feather paint, and leaves no adhesive residue — ensuring a clean, professional result every time. The gold colour also makes it easy to identify during application.

Application Information

Proper Application For Best Results

  • 1

    Surface Preparation

    Ensure surfaces are thoroughly clean, dry, and free from contaminants. For optimal results, clean with isopropyl alcohol and allow to dry completely.

  • 2

    Controlled Application

    Apply with consistent tension, using thumb and forefinger to guide the tape while maintaining light pressure. Avoid stretching, which could cause lifting later.

  • 3

    Edge Sealing

    After application, use a plastic squeegee or your finger to firmly burnish the edges where paint will meet the tape to prevent paint from bleeding underneath.

  • 4

    Removal Technique

    For best results, remove at a 45° angle back upon itself while paint is still slightly tacky (but not wet). This creates the cleanest edge possible for professional results.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does Washi PREMIUM Gold compare to regular masking tapes?

    Washi PREMIUM Gold is significantly superior to standard masking tapes for marine applications. It features Nitto's proprietary Washi paper technology which creates much cleaner paint edges, resists UV exposure for up to 6 months (versus days for standard tapes), eliminates paint bleed-through, and conforms perfectly to complex curves. Additionally, it won't tear during removal and leaves absolutely no residue—key factors when working on high-value yacht surfaces.

  • Can this tape be left in place during curing of two-part marine paints?

    Yes, Washi PREMIUM Gold is specifically engineered to handle the chemical exposure and curing processes of two-part marine paints and clear coats. Unlike standard tapes that can react with paint solvents, this tape maintains its integrity throughout the curing process, allowing for clean removal even after the paint has dried to the perfect consistency. This makes it ideal for multi-stage painting processes common in yacht finishing.

  • What makes this tape suitable for curved surfaces?

    The unique construction of Washi PREMIUM Gold combines a thin 0.09mm profile with a perfectly balanced adhesive system that allows the tape to follow curves without wrinkling or lifting. The special Washi paper backing has directional flexibility without stretching, enabling it to maintain perfect adhesion around complex contours while resisting the tendency to lift at edges that plagues standard masking tapes.

  • Is it worth the higher price compared to standard masking tapes?

    For professional yacht finishing, absolutely. While Washi PREMIUM Gold commands a premium price, it directly translates to substantial time and cost savings by: 1) Eliminating rework from paint bleed-through, 2) Reducing labor through faster, more confident application and removal, 3) Enabling single-pass masking for complex designs, and 4) Preventing costly damage to underlying finishes.
