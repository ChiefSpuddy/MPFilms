General Purpose Surface Protection Film

Hullguard Protect is a versatile surface protection film available in either a clear or blue finish — both with a thickness of 0.05mm and featuring Nitto's trusted Special Adhesive Technology.

Ideal for protecting lacquered and smooth surfaces during construction, refits, transportation, and storage, these films offer strong, reliable adhesion when applied to clean, dry surfaces. They're easy to handle and apply, with a comfortable roll size, low unwind force, and excellent humidity resistance — ensuring they stay in place throughout your project.

For best results, we recommend using our optional MPF application kits, which include a squeegee, film cutters, and core handles for easier installation on angled or curved surfaces.

Application Information

Proper Application For Best Results

  • 1

    Clean the Surface

    Ensure all surfaces are clean, dry, and free from dust or contaminants before application.

  • 2

    Prepare the Film

    Use core handles for easier unrolling, especially when applying to large or angled surfaces.

  • 3

    Apply Gradually

    Start from one end and apply gradually, using a squeegee to remove air bubbles as you go.

  • 4

    Secure the Edges

    Ensure edges are firmly pressed down to prevent lifting or water ingress.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long can Hullguard Protect remain on surfaces?

    Hullguard Protect is designed for temporary protection and performs best when used for up to one month. While it may last longer indoors, the UV resistance is limited to approximately one month for outdoor applications. For longer-term protection, consider our Hullguard Premium range.

  • What's the difference between the clear and blue versions?

    Both versions offer the same level of protection and adhesive performance. The clear version allows visibility of the underlying surface, making it ideal when surface monitoring is needed. The blue version offers better visibility of coverage areas, making it easier to see where protection has been applied, which can be useful during extensive projects.

  • Can Hullguard Protect be applied to all marine surfaces?

    Hullguard Protect is specifically designed for lacquered and smooth surfaces. It performs best on gelcoat, painted surfaces, polished metals, and smooth fiberglass. For textured surfaces, teak, or irregular shapes, we recommend specialized products like Deckguard Teak & Metal Protect or Hullguard Blue Metal+.

  • How do I remove Hullguard Protect when no longer needed?

    Removal is simple—start at one corner and slowly peel the film at a 180-degree angle (back against itself). For large areas, you can cut the film into manageable sections before removal. Hullguard Protect is designed to leave no adhesive residue when properly removed.
