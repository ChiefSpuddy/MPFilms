General Purpose Masking for Short-Term Indoor Use (MPF9393)

The MPF9393 is a natural masking tape designed for basic, non-critical applications where precision isn't essential. Ideal for indoor environments, it's a practical choice for short-term masking during general maintenance, protection, or prep work.

Available in 38mm, 50mm, and 75mm widths (each in 50m rolls), this cream-coloured tape applies smoothly to a variety of surfaces and is easy to remove with minimal residue. It's a cost-effective option when working indoors, although not recommended for marine painting or long-term projects requiring clean lines or weather resistance.

Application Information

Proper Application For Best Results

  • 1

    Surface Preparation

    Ensure surfaces are clean, dry, and free from dust or grease to maximize adhesion and minimize residue on removal.

  • 2

    Ideal Application Temperature

    Apply in room temperature environments (15-25°C) for optimal adhesion performance. Avoid applying to very cold surfaces.

  • 3

    Applying Technique

    Unroll and press firmly as you go, avoiding stretching the tape. Use smooth, continuous motions rather than short sections.

  • 4

    Removal Timing

    For best results, remove within 24-48 hours of application. Leaving the tape in place for extended periods may increase residue upon removal.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is this tape suitable for marine applications?

    This standard masking tape (MPF9393) is not recommended for marine painting or applications where exposure to moisture, UV light, or outdoor conditions is expected. For marine applications, we recommend our specialized marine-grade tapes which offer superior water resistance, UV stability, and clean removal in challenging environments.

  • How long can I leave this tape applied before removing it?

    For optimal results with minimal residue, we recommend removing the tape within 24-48 hours after application. Extended periods, especially in warmer conditions, may result in increased adhesive transfer or difficulty in removal. This tape is designed for short-term indoor applications only.

  • Can this tape be used on delicate surfaces?

    While it has moderate adhesion suitable for many common surfaces, we do not recommend using this standard masking tape on highly delicate or valuable surfaces like historic materials, fine finishes, or freshly painted walls less than 24 hours old. For these applications, we offer specialty low-tack tapes specifically designed for sensitive surfaces.

  • What's the difference between this and higher-grade masking tapes?

    This standard masking tape is a cost-effective solution for basic, non-critical applications. Higher-grade masking tapes typically offer features like sharper paint lines, UV resistance, greater conformability to irregular surfaces, higher temperature resistance, and clean removal after extended periods. The right choice depends on your specific project requirements and quality expectations.
