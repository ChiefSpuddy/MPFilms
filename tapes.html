Standard Masking Tape

This standard natural masking tape (Ref-MPF9393) is cream in colour and is available in a range of different widths and reel lengths for general purpose masking applications, where precision isn't the main factor and where masking is needed for short periods only. These basic tapes are relatively cheap to buy and are typically used indoors. Available from stock in 38mm, 50mm and 75mm (all x 50m in length), minimum order quantity is 36 reels.

They can be applied to various surfaces but users should consider more sophisticated tapes when looking for precision masking, especially in the marine industry.

  • Use: Indoor Only
  • Weatherability: Not for outdoor use
  • Colour: Cream
  • Ease of Application: Good
  • Residue: Minimal
  • Cost effective

Washi INDOOR Yellow

Washi INDOOR Masking tape (Ref-MPF721) has been designed for masking areas that should not be painted, like furniture, fittings and bulkheads during boat manufacture or re-fit. This tape is designed for indoor use - yet is does has some UV-resistance for applications sited on or near to a window.

Again (like the PREMIUM Gold) this tape is produced from fine Japanese flat paper and will give up to 3 weeks weatherability in the event that a vessel has to be moved to different areas of the site for finishing. The pressure sensitive adhesive allows for excellent grip and durability whilst still being easy to remove without residue or tearing.

SUBMERSIBLE+

The Submersible+ tape (Ref-MPF57G) is an anti-corrosion single-sided protective tape which can be used as a protective sheet for mechanical and corrosion protection of various objects with irregular shapes. For example, submersible pumps and electronics - or prop shaft bearings on ships in dry dock.

In other words, it is ideal for the technical and corrosion protection of various kinds of buried objects or submersible items with irregular shapes, including subsea cable splicing and repair. The product has a black EPT-butyl rubber backing, brown petrolatum-butyl rubber self-bonding layer and a white PE liner. This product replaces the 3 steps of the conventional method: the filling materials, the petrolatum tape and the protective tape.

  • Use: An anti corrosion single-sided protective tape
  • Weatherability/UV resist: <6 months
  • Colour: Black/Brown/White
  • Ease of Application: Skilled
  • Perfect watertightness
  • Very strong adhesion level, even for highly irregular shapes
  • Prevents rust
  • Easy to pre-punch
